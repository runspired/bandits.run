# About Bay Bandits

The Bay Bandits is a **trail running community** based in the San Francisco Bay Area, dedicated to bringing runners together for weekly trail adventures.

## Our Mission

We believe in:
- Making trail running accessible to everyone
- Building a supportive community
- Exploring the beautiful trails of the Bay Area
- Having fun while staying fit

## Weekly Runs

We organize regular group runs throughout the week, with options for all fitness levels:

:::tip No-Drop Policy
All our runs follow a no-drop policy, meaning no one gets left behind. We regroup at key points to keep everyone together.
:::

### What to Expect

- **Varied terrain**: From easy fire roads to technical single track
- **Different paces**: Options for social runners and those looking for a workout
- **Friendly atmosphere**: Welcoming to runners of all backgrounds
- **Post-run hangouts**: Optional socializing after the run

:::warning Trail Conditions
Always check the latest trail conditions before heading out. Trails can be muddy after rain!
:::

## Getting Started

New to trail running? No problem! Here's how to join:

1. Check out our schedule on this site
2. Show up at the meeting location 15 minutes early
3. Introduce yourself to the run leaders
4. Choose a pace group that fits your level
5. Have fun!
