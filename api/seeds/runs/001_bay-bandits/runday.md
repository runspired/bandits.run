# RUNDAY - Monday Trail Run

RUNDAY is our signature **Monday evening trail run** at Redwood Regional Park, designed to kick off your week with energy and community.

## About This Run

Every Monday, rain or shine, we gather at Skyline Gate for an evening of trail running through the beautiful redwoods. This run has been a Bay Bandits tradition since the beginning!

### What Makes RUNDAY Special

- **Three pace options** to fit everyone's goals
- **Consistent schedule** - same time, same place every week
- **Beautiful location** in the heart of the East Bay hills
- **Post-run community** time to connect with fellow runners

## Trail Conditions

The trails at Redwood are generally well-maintained but can vary by season:

- **Summer (Jun-Sep)**: Dry and dusty, watch for poison oak
- **Fall (Oct-Nov)**: Perfect conditions!
- **Winter (Dec-Mar)**: Muddy after rain, bring trail shoes with good grip
- **Spring (Apr-May)**: Beautiful wildflowers, some muddy sections
